About me

At Charlotte McCoy Photography, we believe photography is about more than images. It is about preserving feeling. The quiet moments. The unspoken connection. The beauty that exists between people when they are simply being themselves. Photography is more than images. It’s memory, emotion, and connection preserved in time. At Charlotte McCoy Photography, we focus on telling your story naturally. No forced poses. No distractions. Just you, your people, and the moments that matter most.

Every couple, every family, every story is different. That is why we take the time to understand who you are, what matters to you, and how you want your story to be remembered. From the first conversation to the final delivery, we work with care, professionalism, and intention.

From quiet exchanges to joyful celebrations, every photograph is created with intention, care, and a deep respect for your story.
